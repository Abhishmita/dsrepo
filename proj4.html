Project 2

Customer churning Prediction for telecom business

Customer churn prediction is to measure why customers are leaving a business. As per research, the cost of on-boarding a new customer is more than the cost of retaining an old customer.

  • Built an Artificial Neural Network to predict customer churning in telecom business.
  • Engineered features the dataset and set up exploratory data analysis
  • Trained the model to receive an accuracy of 78%.
  • Used precision, recall and f1-score to measure model performance
